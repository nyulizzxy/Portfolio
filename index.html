About Me

Hi! I'm Liz, aka its.lizzxy. I am a huge fan of creating short-form videos that highlight every experience of mine! It stemmed from a fear

of not capturing my memories well enough because my memory is atrocious, and since then, I've implemented it into my work as well.

What started as a little hobby of mine has secured me my current two campus jobs, one as a Visiting Student GEO and another as a

content creator in NYUAD's Student Persistence and Well-Being department. I absolutely LOVE traveling and travel videos are where

I shine the most! My phone is always in my hand, which is usually seen as a bad things, but my friends seem to like that they

always have their own personal photographer and videographer at no cost. Although I must mention that I take forever to get to

editing and finalizing the videos, so the footage is just rotting in my camera roll in the meantime. I have 1TB of storage on my

phone, yet before every event, I have to keep clearing storage. I end up spam posting on social media once I finally get to editing

it, which is reflected in the upload times of the portfolio posts!
